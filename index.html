About Me

Patrick Burke is a student at The Pennsylvania State University pursuing a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on robotics. He also received his Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from The Pennsylvania State University. For his research, he works in the BioRob-InFL Laboratory developing an eel-like swimming robot that will be trained to swim using model-based machine learning. He has worked in product design for manufacturing, manufacturing automation, and mechatronic system design with extensive experience using CAD software to develop parts for manufacturing and training vision systems for automated product inspection. Outside of the lab and the workplace he spends most of his free time 3D printing parts for various engineering projects and building and flying quadcopters.
